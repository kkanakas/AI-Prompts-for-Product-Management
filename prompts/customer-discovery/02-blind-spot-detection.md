# Research Blind Spot Detection

**Phase:** Customer Discovery
**Purpose:** Identify topics, segments, and follow-ups missing from your research.

## Prompt Template

```
Based on my interview transcripts, what topics am I NOT asking about that
might be relevant?
What user segments am I not representing?
What follow-up questions did I fail to ask when interesting topics came up?
```

## Usage Notes

- Attach your interview transcripts or summaries before running
- Works best with 5+ interviews so the AI can spot gaps
- Run this mid-research (not just at the end) to course-correct early
