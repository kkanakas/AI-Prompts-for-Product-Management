# Structured Market Analysis

**Phase:** Market Research
**Purpose:** Analyze market data with rich context and cross-referenced sources.

## Prompt Template

```
Context:
We are building [PRODUCT DESCRIPTION] targeting [TARGET MARKET].
We are considering [STRATEGIC QUESTION].
I have attached:
- [LIST ATTACHED DOCUMENTS: analyst reports, reviews, survey results, etc.]

Questions:
1. How do analysts describe [OPPORTUNITY/TREND]?
2. What do users say they like/dislike?
3. Does this [FEATURE/DIRECTION] align with market trends or differ?
4. Is this [FEATURE/DIRECTION] table stakes or differentiator in our segment?
```

## Placeholders

| Variable | Description |
|---|---|
| `[PRODUCT DESCRIPTION]` | What you're building and for whom |
| `[TARGET MARKET]` | Company size, industry, geography |
| `[STRATEGIC QUESTION]` | The decision you're trying to inform |

## Key Principles

- **Provide rich context** — don't just upload raw data; frame what you're trying to understand
- **Cross-reference multiple sources** — patterns across sources are more trustworthy than any single source
- **Attach real data** — analyst reports, competitor reviews, customer surveys
