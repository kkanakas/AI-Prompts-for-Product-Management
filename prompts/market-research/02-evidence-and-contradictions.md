# Evidence and Contradictions Check

**Phase:** Market Research
**Purpose:** Stress-test a market finding by asking AI to show supporting and contradicting evidence.

## Prompt Template

```
You identified [FEATURE OPPORTUNITY] as a key unmet need.
1. What evidence supports this? (specific quotes/data)
2. What evidence contradicts it?
3. Which sources mention this vs. do not?
4. How do I validate whether this is a real need or just what people say?
```

## Placeholders

| Variable | Description |
|---|---|
| `[FEATURE OPPORTUNITY]` | The opportunity or finding you want to pressure-test |

## Limitations to Keep in Mind

- **Surface-level only** — AI sees public info, not internal strategy or customer data
- **Recency gaps** — Public info may lag behind actual competitor progress
- **No strategic context** — AI can identify positioning but can't evaluate conversion rates or profitability
